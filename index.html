Hi there, I’m Caroline. I’m a Graphic Designer within the Twin Cities area. In May 2020, I graduated with a BA in Graphic Design. Throughout my coursework I focused on areas such as brand identity, web design, and UX/UI. Outside of my major classes I focused on areas such as communications, marketing, and photography. To further my education, I enrolled in a UX/UI certification program at the University of Minnesota.

Currently, I am trying to start a side business of creating wedding save the dates and wedding invites. In the future I would love to start a small business doing invites and floral design. Please check out my “wedding” page if you are interested in learning more.
